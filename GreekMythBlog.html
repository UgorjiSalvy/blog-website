PRIMORDIALS

They were the first deities the greeks believed to have emerged at the beginning of creation,
the were purely elemental beings, and were the strongest amongst any other greek god/goddess.

These are the top ten strongest Primordials:
  • Chaos: Personification of Chaos, Space, Void, Force, Infinity of space, Air,
    Creation & Destruction, Mixture of all Primevial Elements.
    Came out of nothing, First deity to emerge at creation.
  • Nyx: Personification of Night, Terror, Shadow, Dark, Gloom.
    Resides below the arch of the heveanly dome, in the lower realm
    Daughter of Chaos, wife-sister of Erebus.
  • Hemera: Personification of Day, Dawn, Light, Sun.
    Rises up from the ends of the Earth to scatter the mist of Night(Nyx), to reveal the shining Heaven(Aether).
    Daughter of Nyx, wife-sister Aether.
  • Aether: Personification of Bright glowing Atmosphere of the upper heaven, Sky, Heaven, Light.
    Resides between the arch of Heaven dome, high above the lower Realms.
    Husband-brother of Hemera.
  • Erebus: Personification of Darkness, Gloom, Shadow, Mist of Darkness.
    Sunk in the hollows of the earth, Encircling the dismal realm of the Underworld.
    Husband-brother of Nyx.
  • Gaea: Popularly known as MOTHER EARTH,
    Personification of Earth, Fertility, Foundation of the Universe, Earth's Ecosystem, Athmosphere, Earth's Core&Crust.
    Emerging from the earth, never apart from her primevial element
    Mother-wife of Uranus.
  • Eros: Personification of Love, Procreation, Life, Life drive, Generation.
    Resides in the dark void of Space, putting the Universe in Order
    Uses his Silver Bow and Arrow, to put love in the heart of other primordials for procreation,
    Depicted as a handsome man with golden wings, and an eye patch(Blindness of love) in one eye.
  • Tartarus: Personification of Abyss, Pit, Death.
    Being the Underworld or Netherland Himself
    As the Anti-heaven(Aether), Resides below the roots/foundation of the earth, In the Underworld.
  • Uranus: Personification of Starry, Cloud filled sky, Heavens.
    Husband-son of Gaea, Resides just above the Heavenly dome, representing their Union.
  • Typhon: Personification of Volcanoes, Destruction, Hurricanes, Tornadoes, Storms.
    Took more than 12 Olympians, Even with the 3 strongest to lock him up.
    Resides in mount Etna(currently) as a prisoner, In a whirlwind(fromerly) as the storm itself.
    With his primordial wife Echidna, was the father of all Monsters. He himself a very frightening monster.

TITANS

They were the second set of deities the greeks believed to have emerged after the beginning of creation,
the were lords of the Elements, so therefore where not fully elemental beings like the primordials, and they are not to be thought of as gods but as lords.

These are the top twelve most important Titans:
  • Cronos: Lords of Time, Agriculture, Sky.
    Brother-husband to Rhea(Marriage).
  • Rhea: Titaness of Agriculture, Fertility, Marriage, Childbirth.
    Sister-wife to Cronos(Time).
  • Oceanus: Lord of Oceans, Seas, and Storms
    Brother-husband to Tethys(Rivers).
  • Hyperion: Lord of heat, light, brilliance, fire.
    Brother-husband to Theia(Sky).
  • Iapetus: Lord of Night, Twilight, Moon, Midnight, Dusk.
    Married to Clymene(Fame, Glory), One of the 3000 Oceanids.
  • Tethys: Fertile Waters, Rivers, Waters, Ponds, Dam.
    Sister-wife to Oceanus(Oceans).
  • Krius: Lord of Constellations, Stars, Astronomy.
    Married to Hecate(Magic).
  • Coeus: Lord of intellect, Heavens Axis, Knowledge, Thought, Cleverness
    Brother-husband to Phoebe(Moon).
  • Mnemosyne: Titan lordess of memory, mind, languages, prophecy.
  • Themis: Lordess of Divine Justice, Order, Advice, Reproach.
  • Theia: Lordess of Sky, Light, Crystals, Precious gems and stones.
    Sister-wife to Hyperion(Heat).
  • Phoebe: Lordess of Moon, Fertility, Youth, Birth.
    Sister-wife to Coeus(Intellect).
